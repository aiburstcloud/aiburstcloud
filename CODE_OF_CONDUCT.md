# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the project and community
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly.
